Artist Statement

I use 3d animation technique to convey my feelings and thoughts. I try to convey my feelings to people by taking nature and life as an example in my works. I am currently working on character design, character animation, visual effects and I continue to improve myself. I want to produce films that will affect people's feelings and make them question some subjects by constantly improving and learning.
